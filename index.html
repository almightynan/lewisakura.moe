More?

Nice to see your curiosity! I was 18 years old when this site was last updated, and I have a huge interest in technology. Whilst software engineering is my passion and my major, I've dabbled in network engineering and cybersecurity. They're really fun topics, so I'm very passionate about all three!

I really like designing pretty websites, but actually implementing them is a problem for me. I've unfortunately had to throw away projects because of it mainly because they were web design heavy or they depended on public usage. However, some projects I've been able to keep because of being in teams! I really like working in teams of people since it helps me distribute the workload and I can focus on my strengths, which is usually engineering backend technology.

I'm also a game developer, primarily working on the Roblox platform. Despite the surrounding stigma, I think it's a great platform for learning, and I've had a lot of fun working on it. I've also used Unity, Unreal, and Godot.

In terms of being a content creator, I have always had an interest in providing entertainment to people. One day, when I have a bit of money to spare, I'd like to invest in becoming a VTuber – I have a character already, just not the money to bring it into existence. From there, depending on how it goes, I might focus completely on content creation and move my engineering work to the side. Of course, I'd still partake in programming, it has been my life after all, but I'd like to focus more on entertaining people and making them happy. I love seeing people smile.

My name is Lewis Crichton. That's my full name.

Here's some quickfire fun facts about this website:

  • I wrote this in approximately 2 hours.

  • Speaking of build-time tools, there are none here. I manually wrote all the HTML because I'm a masochist and I haven't futureproofed this at all.
  • The monospace font is Iosevka (specifically the web variant), and everything else is Inter, unless of course none of it loaded.
